About Me

Hello, everyone! My name is Anthony Benavente. I was born on April 6, 1995 on the island of Saipan, the capital of the Northern Mariana Islands which is a territory of the United States. When I was only ten years old, my family and I moved across the pond to Raleigh, North Carolina, so that my parents could obtain their masters from North Carolina State University. All my life, I thought I was going to follow my parents' footsteps and attend North Carolina State as well to become an architect. However, after taking a semester of art at Green Hope High School, Cary, North Carolina, I quickly realized that the dream of becoming an architect was impossible. I then adjusted my sights when I had recieved my first laptop as a gift from my grandmother ( this was my first laptop for those curious) and decided to dabble in the world of programming and computer science. Here is where I found my niche, my calling, my future.

Today, I am attending Western Carolina University studying Computer Science and Applied Mathematics. I plan on graduating with my undergraduate degree in 2017 after which I will continue on in life, fufilling my dream of working in the industry as a computer programmer. However, one of my future goals in life is to obtain my Master's degree, PhD. and become a professor at a university.
